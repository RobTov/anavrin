No Willpower? Enlist the Help of an App.

Apps like Focus Booster and AntiSocial have your back! AntiSocial blocks your access to a selection of websites with a timer that you select. Focus Booster is a mobile phone app that relies on the Pomodoro Technique, where you work intensively for 25 minutes and then you break for five minutes. The app also includes productivity reports and revenue charts.
