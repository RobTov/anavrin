Look at cuddly animal pictures.

This is hardcore science. If you have a problem with this, then you have a problem with science itself (j/k). But for real–a Japanese study measured performance after looking at cute animals. Yes, they actually did this. Here’s a quote from the study: “Results show that participants performed tasks requiring focused attention more carefully after viewing cute images.” Can you believe it?
