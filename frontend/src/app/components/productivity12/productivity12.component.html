Throw stuff away and declutter.

12. Throw stuff away and declutter. Did you know your physical environment impacts your work and productivity? And I don’t mean just if you work in a “cool” office or not. Depending on your company and your role in the company, that’s largely out of your control. But you can control your workspace. It helps you be more productive when you don’t have to hunt and peck for whatever that missing thing is. You’ll save time by not doing that. You’ll also get more clarity and focus for your tasks at hand. Check out more tips here.
